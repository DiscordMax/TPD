Information

We are New Jersey State Roleplay's largest Department, the Trenton Police Department is the municipal law enforcement agency responsible for enforcing the law and maintaining public order within Trenton. The department currently employs approximately eighty officers and is structured into numerous bureaus. Join our Discord.

Bureaus

We have many bureaus, which all offer extra roleplay opportunities within the department. In each bureau, you will have the opportunity to apply, gain knowledge, and traverse your way through the many ranks offered to you. Application links and descriptions of each bureau are listed below.

Criminal Investigations Bureau

Detectives assigned to the Criminal Investigations Bureau are tasked with investigating and acting on crimes and criminal organizations that operate within the city.

Special Operations Bureau

Officers assigned to the Special Operations Bureau are tasked with providing protection, support, security, firepower, and rescue to police operations in high-risk situations, where specialized tactics are necessary to minimize casualties.

Internal Affairs Bureau

Officers assigned to the Internal Affairs Unit are tasked with investigating complaints and overseeing the conduct of officers.

Training and Admissions Bureau

Officers assigned to the Training and Admissions Bureau are tasked with admitting and training applicants.

Public Relations Bureau

Officers assigned to the Public Relations Bureau are tasked with creating reports, coordinating events, and giving the department a better image.

Administrative Services Bureau

Command members assigned to the Administrative Services Bureau are tasked with completing tasks and managing administrative work within the Department. You are required to be a command member to be in this bureau.
